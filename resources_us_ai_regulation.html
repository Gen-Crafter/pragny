Executive Order on Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy AI

On October 30, 2023, President Biden issued a landmark Executive Order establishing new standards for AI safety and security. This comprehensive framework addresses:

Safety & Security
  • ✓ AI system testing requirements
  • ✓ Cybersecurity standards
  • ✓ Critical infrastructure protection
Privacy Protection

  • ✓ Privacy-preserving AI
  • ✓ Consumer protection measures

  • ✓ Bias prevention
  • ✓ Fair AI deployment
  • ✓ Civil rights protection
