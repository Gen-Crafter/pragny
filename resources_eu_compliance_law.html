EU AI Act

Get to know the first-ever regulation focusing on AI risk and compliance in Europe.

What is the EU AI Act?

The EU AI Act is the first legal framework globally to regulate artificial intelligence technologies. It ensures that AI systems developed and deployed within the European Union are safe, transparent, and compliant with ethical standards.

Key Resources

  • Official EU AI Act Proposal
  • European Approach to Artificial Intelligence
  • EU AI Act FAQ

Why Does it Matter?

The regulation aims to foster trust in AI systems by categorizing them based on risk levels and enforcing strict standards for high-risk AI applications. It impacts businesses, developers, and end-users alike.
