About Pragny

Your Partner in Crafting and Governing a Trustworthy AI Future

Pragny is an AI agent and compliance platform offering both cloud-based SaaS and on-premise solutions to help organizations adopt AI securely, ethically, and transparently. From startups to large enterprises, we provide a suite of tools designed to manage the full AI lifecycle—from discovery and risk classification to continuous monitoring and audit readiness.

Our platform supports compliance with leading global standards including the EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and frameworks across India, the USA, Europe, Japan, Singapore, UAE, and Australia.

With advanced features like AI Tracker for Shadow AI Detection, Bias & Fairness Analysis, and Security & Risk Mitigation, Pragny helps AI teams, regulators, and leadership collaborate with shared visibility and agent.

We’re proud to be recognized by Tracxn as the #1 Responsible AI agent company in Asia and ranked 12th globally—a testament to our commitment to building AI that’s safe, fair, and compliant.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower organizations across the globe to build, monitor, and scale AI systems that are not only intelligent — but also ethical, secure, and compliant.

We are committed to shaping a future where AI is governed with integrity and responsibility. Through our suite of AI agent tools — including AI Lifecycle agent, Risk Mitigation, Bias Analysis, and AI Activity Tracking — we help businesses stay aligned with global regulatory frameworks like the EU AI Act and NIST.

Whether cloud-based or on-premise, our solutions support transparency, reduce risk, and enable trustworthy innovation across enterprises, governments, and critical sectors.

Our Vision

We envision a world where AI is deployed responsibly, ensuring fairness, transparency, and security for all users. By empowering organizations with cutting-edge AI agent tools, we aim to drive trust and accountability in AI systems across the globe.

Our Core Values

At Pragny, our values serve as the foundation of how we design, build, and deliver AI agent solutions — enabling responsible AI transformation for enterprises and society.

Trust by Design

We embed trust into every layer of AI development — from model transparency to policy enforcement — enabling safer AI ecosystems by default.

Compliance-Driven Innovation

We empower innovation while ensuring alignment with global regulations like the EU AI Act, NIST, and ISO standards — transforming agent into a growth driver.

Bias-Free Intelligence

Accountable AI

We enable traceability and auditability in every AI interaction — creating strong audit trails and documentation to support internal and external accountability.

Customer-First agent

We tailor every solution to our clients’ risk appetite, industry standards, and deployment needs — from cloud-native to on-premise control.

Sustainability & Security

We promote green AI practices and secure AI lifecycles — minimizing environmental impact and ensuring data confidentiality at scale.
